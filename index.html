Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2025

Curator YouTube Insights ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your YouTube data when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

YouTube Data (OAuth Scope: youtube.readonly)

With your permission, we access the following YouTube information:

  • Your public channel information (name, video count, subscriber count)
  • Recently watched videos
  • Liked videos
  • Subscribed channels

We do not collect your email, password, or allow any action (posting, liking, subscribing) on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your YouTube data to:

  • Generate a personal overview of your viewing activity
  • Display your favorite content and channels
  • Offer insights such as interests and media preferences using internal AI
  • (Optional) Allow you to share your activity with friends or the public
  • Recommend content or connections based on your interests (future feature)

3. Your Privacy Settings

You are in control of your data. You may choose:

  • Private: No data sharing, viewable only to you
  • Internal Only: Data is used for AI insights, not shared
  • Friends Only: Activity viewable by approved friends
  • Public: Data visible on your public profile

You can update your preferences at any time in the appâ€™s YouTube Data Settings panel.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is securely stored with encryption and access controls. You may request deletion of your data at any time via your profile settings. We retain data only as long as needed to provide the services you opt into.

5. Revoking Access

You may revoke YouTube access at any time by:

  • Using the Curator YouTube Insights settings
  • Visiting your Google Account at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

Once access is revoked, we will no longer retrieve new data and will delete existing data upon request.

6. Third-Party Access

We do not share, sell, or trade your data with third parties. All data use remains within the Curator ecosystem for the purposes described above.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. Users will be notified of major changes. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
